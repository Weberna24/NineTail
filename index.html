INTRODUCING: Ninetails Coffee

Step beyond the ordinary and enter a realm where every sip unleashes bold character. Ninetails Coffee Bar is more than just a café—it’s an experience inspired by ancient legend and modern passion. A place where the mythical elegance of the nine-tailed fox meets the warmth and ritual of artisan coffee.
At Ninetails, every cup is brewed with precision, care, and a touch of the extraordinary. We source only the highest-grade, sustainably harvested beans from around the globe and roast them in-house to capture their full spirit.

Where Flavor Awakens the Spirit

The moment you walk through our doors, you’re transported. Our interior is a fusion of mystic East Asian folklore and sleek, modern design—burnished golds, warm woods, soft lantern lighting, and nine flowing tails woven into our murals and décor. Every corner is designed for comfort, creativity, and connection.

At the heart of Ninetails Coffee Bar is a relentless commitment to quality. Our beans are carefully sourced from ethical farms across the globe, each chosen for their unique flavor profiles and grown with respect for the land. Every batch is roasted in-house in small quantities to ensure peak freshness and richness. Whether you prefer a bold espresso with layers of complexity or a velvety latte with house-made syrups, our baristas take the time to make each drink just right. But Ninetails isn’t just about what’s in your cup. Our light meal offerings are crafted to complement our beverages and satisfy your hunger without weighing you down. Bagels are baked fresh each morning and served with a variety of spreads and toppings

A Space Like No Other

At Ninetails, we believe in slowing down, savoring the moment, and finding beauty in the details. From the flick of foam in your cappuccino to the welcoming smile behind the counter, everything here is done with intention. It’s a place where quality meets atmosphere, where tradition meets creativity, and where every visit feels a little magical.
